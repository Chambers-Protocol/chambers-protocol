# Chambers Protocol - Commercial Access Tiers

The Chambers Protocol operates on a "Credit Consumption" backend, but Enterprise contracts are structured as flat-rate Annual Licenses to ensure predictable billing and uninterrupted operations.

## 1. The Pilot Node (Validation Tier)
Designed for a single innovation team to test the protocol's efficacy on live strategic problems.
* **Duration:** One-Time Fee
* **Credit Allocation:** 25,000 Credits
* **Pricing:** $1,000
* **Deployment:** Standard Windows Installer
* **Support:** Standard Email Support

## 2. The Divisional Node (Operational Tier)
Designed for high-velocity decision-making units (e.g., The Strategy Office, C-Suite, or R&D). This tier provides enough operational bandwidth for daily usage by 50+ power users.
* **Duration:** Annual Contract
* **Credit Allocation:** 2,000,000 Credits / Year
* **Overage Protection:** Soft-cap (Service is not interrupted; account manager is notified)
* **Pricing:** Annual License Fee
* **Support:** Priority Support & Integration Guide

## 3. The Enterprise Grid (Infrastructure Tier)
Designed for organization-wide deployment. This turns the Protocol into a standard utility available to every employee with a Claude Enterprise seat.
* **Duration:** Multi-Year Contract
* **Credit Allocation:** Unrestricted (100M+ Credits)
* **Pricing:** Custom Volume Licensing
* **Deployment:** Silent Install Support (SCCM/Intune) for IT Teams
* **Support:** Dedicated Account Manager
