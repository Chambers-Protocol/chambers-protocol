### Tier 2: The Infrastructure Layer (Digital Physics)
*Use these when digital systems, security, or compute power are the constraints.*
* **`cloud_ops_audit`**: For system uptime, latency, SRE metrics, and digital overhead.
* **`cyber_shield_audit`**: For defense posture, threat surface, and resilience.
* **`research_hpqai`**: For computational breakthroughs, quantum readiness, and "black box" innovation potential.

### Tier 3: The Strategic Layer (Abstract Physics)
*Use these when human behavior, money, or market forces are the constraints.*
* **`risk_mechanics`**: The "Master Node." Use this to calculate the probability of failure for ANY complex system (Financial, Operational, or Strategic).
* **`venture_viability`**: For valuation, market velocity, and team composition (M&A, Startups).
* **`product_strategy`**: For the "Idea to Exit" pipeline, product-market fit, and execution roadmaps.
* **`cognitive_psych`**: For profiling decision-makers, leadership psychology, and bias detection.
